Meet ANA!

Driven by creativity and a passion for innovation, I view each project as an opportunity to transform spaces into personalized experiences that enhance quality of life.

Originally from Madeira, Portugal, I chose Milan as my new home to immerse myself in the vibrant heart of the design world, an ever-evolving environment.

Education

Civil Engineer (2010) | Interior Designer at Istituto Marangoni Milano (2023)

Specialty

Blending natural and sustainable elements with modern design

My work in residential developments has enhanced my ability to blend natural and sustainable elements, achieving a balance between modern design and the surrounding environment. This approach allows me to create spaces that serve as peaceful sanctuaries while remaining highly functional.
